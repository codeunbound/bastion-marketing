Billing questions

Good to know.

How does device counting work?

You're billed for the number of devices you actively protect. Family covers up to 5 devices. Business starts at ₹599/month for the first 10 devices and scales with a small per-device fee up to 29. Reach 30 devices and Enterprise becomes the better fit.

What's the difference between shared cloud and a dedicated VPS?

Family and Business plans run on Bastion's secure, shared cloud instance — fast to set up and maintenance-free. Enterprise runs on a dedicated, isolated VPS provisioned just for you, giving full traffic isolation and data control for compliance-sensitive work.

Can I switch plans or billing periods later?

Yes. You can move between plans as your needs change, and switch between monthly and annual billing at any renewal. Annual billing saves roughly 20% versus paying monthly.

How do I pay, and do I get an invoice?

Family plans are paid monthly via UPI. Business and Enterprise plans include GST invoicing for your records. Get in touch and we'll set you up.

Not sure which plan fits?

Tell us how many devices you need to protect and we'll point you to the right plan.
