How it works

Set up in minutes, not an afternoon.

Bastion routes each device's DNS through a filter you control. Nothing to plug in, nothing to root or jailbreak.

STEP 01

Add a device

Name the phone, tablet or laptop you want to protect. Bastion creates a private, per-device address so each one is managed on its own.

STEP 02

Apply the profile

Scan a QR or download a one-tap profile. Guided steps for iOS, Android (Stock, MIUI, OneUI, ColorOS), macOS and Windows.

STEP 03

Manage from anywhere

Toggle categories, block a specific site, and review the last 7 days of activity — all from a clean dashboard on your own phone.

Straight talk

What Bastion can and can't do.

Filtering is a strong layer, not a magic wall. We'd rather you know exactly where the edges are.

What it does well
  • Blocks entire categories and specific sites across every device, on Wi-Fi and mobile data.
  • Neutralises Apple iCloud Private Relay so it can't quietly route around your rules.
  • Locks tight on company-managed iPhones — MDM profiles can't be removed by the user.
  • Gives you a clear, per-device record of what was reached and what was blocked.
Where the limits are
  • Someone who can change a device's DNS setting can bypass filtering — protect it with a Screen Time or device PIN.
  • A VPN app installed on the device tunnels around DNS. Restrict VPN installs to keep rules intact.
  • On personal (BYOD) devices, enforcement relies on trust — company-issued devices give the strongest control.

A bypass-resistant Android app is on our roadmap.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install an app on every device?

No. Bastion works by changing a device's DNS settings — on iOS and macOS you install a small configuration profile, and on Android you set a Private DNS hostname. We give you guided, device-specific steps for each one.

Does it work on mobile data, or only Wi-Fi?

Both. Because filtering happens at the DNS level on the device itself, your rules follow the device whether it's on home Wi-Fi, school Wi-Fi or cellular data.

Can my child or employee just turn it off?

On a device where they can freely change DNS settings, yes — so we recommend protecting those settings with a Screen Time passcode (iOS) or device restrictions (Android). On company-managed iPhones enrolled via MDM, the profile cannot be removed by the user.

What's the difference between the business and enterprise plans?

Business plans (SME) run on a shared, secure cloud instance and cover up to 29 devices with per-employee reporting. Enterprise gives you a dedicated, isolated VPS with filtering beyond DNS — built for compliance-sensitive teams.

How is my data handled?

We keep only what's needed to show you the dashboard — a rolling 7-day query history per device. Enterprise customers can run on their own isolated instance for full data control.
