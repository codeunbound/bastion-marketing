Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

What Bastion is

Bastion is a DNS-based content filtering service. When a device uses Bastion, its DNS queries (domain lookups) pass through our servers so we can apply filtering rules you configure.

What we collect

Data Why Retention
Email address Account login & support contact Until account deletion
DNS query logs Show you per-device activity in the dashboard 7 days (rolling)
Device names You assign these to identify devices Until you remove the device
Payment info Processed by Razorpay; we store order IDs only Until account deletion

What we do NOT collect

  • Page content, URLs, or form data — DNS sees only the domain name, not the full URL or page contents.
  • IP addresses for tracking — we do not profile or sell browsing data.
  • Data from other apps — Bastion operates at the DNS layer only.

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and basic profile (name, profile picture) from Google. We use the email to create or match your Bastion account. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, Drive, or any other Google service.

Third-party services

  • Razorpay — payment processing (India). Subject to Razorpay's privacy policy.
  • Google Identity Services — optional sign-in. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

Data security

All traffic between your devices and Bastion is encrypted (DNS-over-HTTPS or DNS-over-TLS). Account data is stored in an encrypted-at-rest database. Access is restricted to the service operator.

Your rights

  • Export: Request a copy of your data at any time.
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from the app dashboard, or by emailing us.
  • Correct: Update your email or device names at any time.

Children

Bastion is operated by parents or IT administrators on behalf of minors. We do not collect data directly from children. The account holder (parent or admin) controls all devices and data.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify you via the email on your account at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@bastion.codeunbound.dev.
